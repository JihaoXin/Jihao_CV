\begin{rubric}{Selected Projects}
\entry*[idea2paper.org]%
	\textbf{Idea2Paper --- Turn a Research Idea into a Paper by One Click} (\href{https://idea2paper.org}{idea2paper.org}).
	\par Creator; designed and built the full stack from scratch. An end-to-end \textbf{Research-as-a-Service} system that automates the full research lifecycle --- literature review, experiments, figures, writing, and iterative review --- with no environment setup and human-in-the-loop at any step. Under the hood: multi-agent orchestration (6 specialized agents), automated LaTeX typesetting, dynamic skill loading, state management, context anchoring against long-horizon drift, citation verification, and native Slurm/AWS/GCP/Azure compute. To my knowledge the most complete such system available today, and already used to produce real papers. Open-source (Apache-2.0); built with Claude Code / Gemini CLI.
